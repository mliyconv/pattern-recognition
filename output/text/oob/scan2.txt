Establishment

The Secretary of Commerce, having determined it is in the public interest in connection with the
performance of duties imposed on the Department by law and with the concurrence of the
General Services Administration, established the National Oceanic and Atmospheric
Administration (NOAA) Advisory Committee on Commercial Remote Sensing (ACCRES),
pursuant to the F edera] Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C., App. This Committee serves as a
component of the President's National Space Policy, which encourages U.S. commercial
leadership in space activities.
